      🚨 Emergency Care Information

      Recognize urgent warning signs

      ❤️ Chest Pain

      Sudden chest pain may indicate a heart emergency. Seek immediate medical attention.

      🫁 Breathing Difficulty

      Severe shortness of breath requires urgent care. Call emergency services immediately.

      🧠 Stroke Symptoms

      Face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty. Time is critical.

      🩸 Severe Bleeding

      Apply direct pressure and seek emergency help.

      ⚠️ Unconsciousness

      Loss of consciousness is a medical emergency. Call emergency services.

      🚑 Emergency Reminder

      If symptoms are severe or life-threatening, contact local emergency services immediately.
